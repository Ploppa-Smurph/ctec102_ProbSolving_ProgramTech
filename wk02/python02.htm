Formatting some floating points

        format(1234.567, '.2f')  -> result: 1234.57 -> rounds up and displays 2 decimal
        format(1234.567, ',.0f') -> result: 1,235  -> commas with no digit after decimal
        format(34.567, '8.2f') -> result: '    34.57' -> eight character minimum, two digit after decimal (for making large and small numbers fit in formatted tables)
        format(0.63, '%') -> result: '63.0000000%' -> long percent notation
        format(0.63, '.0%') -> '63%' -> percent notation, no digit after decimal
there are tons of different ways to format, just look in the help and don't get overwhelmed
